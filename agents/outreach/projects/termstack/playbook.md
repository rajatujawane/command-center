Payment Customization Functions. Plus-only.
Static net terms are native on all paid plans since Summer '26 — NEVER pitch those,
never lead with what Shopify does natively, never mention Summer '26 / Winter '26 /
Editions in outreach.

## Sender identity
From: rajat@varrlabshq.com, from-name "Rajat from TermStack".
Never termstack.com or brandzway.com.

## Sequence
3 touches only: T1 day 0, T2 +4d, T3 +9d (T3 is the FINAL touch and carries a
graceful close). No reply by T3 + 7d -> dropped, silently. NEVER a 4th email.
Any reply at all -> Rajat responds within 4 hours (flag ⚡ in the summary the
moment it is detected). Replied thread = links now allowed.

## Hard gates (drop, don't stretch)
- Shopify Plus confirmed or no contact.
- Real wholesale/B2B channel or no contact. No D2C-only stores.
- Persona: 1) Ecom Manager / Head of Ecom, 2) Director/VP Ecom, 3) Founder if <$5M,
  4) Ops lead. NEVER VP Marketing / CMO.

## Tone (strict, Rajat's voice, send-ready)
Plain short sentences. NO em dashes, no hyphenated compound adjectives; commas,
colons, periods only. No exclamation marks, no marketing adjectives, no AI phrases
("hope this finds you well", "circling back", "touching base", "I wanted to reach
out", "seamless", "streamline", "leverage"). Human enough that someone would
believe a person wrote it. UK amounts as GBP.

## Send-window hints (informational only, shown next to each draft in the summary)
US: 6:30-9:30pm IST · UK/EU: 1:30-3pm IST · AU: 6-8am IST. Tue-Fri for T1s.

## Reply branches (all ON DEMAND — Rajat asks, then we draft)
- Yes to walkthrough -> flag "RECORD LOOM TODAY" at the top of the summary with the
  matching archetype below; demo link goes in the replied thread.
- "Who is TermStack" -> one paragraph + app store + YouTube links.
- Soft no -> polite one-line close, mark dropped.
- Hard no -> drop instantly, status do_not_contact.

## The 3 archetype demos (record only after a yes, ~15 min each)
1. DEALER/TIER: tier new = pay now; approved = Net 30; volume + order > $5k =
   Net 60 + 25% deposit. (auto, cycling, outdoor, trade supply)
2. ORDER VALUE: < $1k = pay now; $1k-$10k = Net 30; > $10k = Net 60 + 25% deposit.
   (medical, industrial, equipment)
3. BOUTIQUE WHOLESALE: first-order tag = pay now; established = Net 30;
   established + > $10k = Net 60 + deposit. (fashion, F&B, gifts)

new trigger. Row notes override (e.g. Gesswein: Sep 15, Greg only). Read notes
before every queue decision.
