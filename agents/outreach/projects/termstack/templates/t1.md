# T1 — opener (new thread, ~90 words, ZERO links)

Subject: `{Company}'s wholesale checkout` — or a close company-specific variant.
Never pitchy.

```
Hi {FirstName},

{HOOK: one specific line from the prospect's evidence field. Their wholesale page,
the B2B app they reviewed, the case study or blog they appeared in. Never generic.}

Curious - when {their buyers: clinics, dealers, stockists...} place orders, are
payment terms applied automatically at checkout, or does someone still review
orders or create draft orders?

We built TermStack for exactly this: rules like {approved buyers in their
language} get Net 30 while new accounts pay a deposit on larger {orders in their
language}, applied automatically at checkout.

A US toy & gift wholesaler on Shopify Plus uses it to give established customers
Net terms while requiring deposits from new accounts (5 stars on the App Store).

Rajat
Founder, TermStack by Varr Labs
```

Rules: the qualifying question is the ONLY ask. NO links anywhere, including the
signature (no apps.shopify.com URL, Gmail auto-links plain domains). No demo link,
no setup offer line (the setup offer lives in T2). The rules sentence and the
question must both be in the prospect's buyer language (clinics / dealers /
stockists / labs), not generic "customers". Currency matches their HQ.
