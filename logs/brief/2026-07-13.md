💡 Shop User metafields in checkout Functions is an untouched primitive for B2B credit gating — TermStack could store net terms tier or deposit eligibility as a Shop User metafield and read it directly inside a payment customization Function, eliminating the per-merchant customer tag schemas that make dynamic payment terms fragile today.
