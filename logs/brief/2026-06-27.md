## Shopify

- Shopify Scripts are deprecated on June 30 — three days away. Any Plus store that hasn't migrated custom discount, shipping, or payment logic to Functions will silently revert to Shopify's defaults at checkout with no warning to the merchant. (https://shopify.dev/changelog/shopify-scripts-will-be-deprecated-on-june-30-2026)

- Shopify now handles the EU's new €3 per-tariff-line customs duty automatically for merchants using Managed Markets or import tax/duty calculation — the fee kicks in July 1 on orders under €150 shipped into the EU from outside. A separate handling fee (~€2/parcel) follows by November. (https://documentation-changelog.shopifysvc.com/posts/new-3-eu-import-customs-duty-arrives-july-1)

- Starting with Admin API 2026-10, carrier services created via GraphQL or REST will no longer be auto-added to the General shipping profile — apps have to explicitly wire rates to a profile post-creation, or merchants won't see carrier rates at checkout. (https://shopify.dev/changelog/carrier-services-will-no-longer-be-automatically-added-to-the-default-shipping-profile)

- Per-market settings now extend to cancellation rules alongside return rules — both configurable per market — letting Plus merchants set EU-specific right-of-withdrawal policies without a separate workaround. (https://changelog.shopify.com/)

💡 The Scripts-to-Functions migration is almost entirely focused on D2C discount logic, but the payment-filtering use case — blocking net terms or deferred payment methods for certain buyer segments — is mostly undocumented and untooled. TermStack could publish a concrete migration template showing how to replicate Scripts-era payment method filtering using Functions, capturing the exact Plus stores scrambling this week.

## AI · SaaS · Tech

It traces data flows across files to surface vulnerabilities rather than matching signatures — found 500+ bugs in open-source codebases during internal testing. Team and Max plan access coming soon.
